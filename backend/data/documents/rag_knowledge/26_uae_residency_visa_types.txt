UAE RESIDENCY VISA OPTIONS — COMPLETE GUIDE
============================================

1. EMPLOYMENT VISA (Most Common)
Sponsored by UAE employer.
Duration: 2-3 years (renewable)
Benefits: Work, live, sponsor immediate family
Requirements: Job offer, medical test, Emirates ID

2. INVESTOR VISA / GOLDEN VISA (Property Route)
Property investment: AED 2,000,000+ (fully paid)
Duration: 10 years (renewable)
Benefits: No sponsor needed, sponsor unlimited family, no need to return every 6 months
Process: Title deed → DLD valuation → GDRFA or ICA application → Medical → Emirates ID

3. ENTREPRENEUR / STARTUP VISA
Approved startup: AREA2071, Dubai Future Accelerators, Hub71 (Abu Dhabi)
OR: AED 500,000 investment in approved business
Duration: 5 years

4. FREELANCER VISA
UAE freelancer permit + residency
Available through: DTCM, Dubai Media City, Dubai Internet City, Fujairah Creative City
Cost: AED 7,500 – 15,000/year
Income requirement: AED 360,000/year (some free zones)
Best for: Consultants, content creators, developers

5. RETIREMENT VISA
Age 55+
Options (meet ONE):
- AED 2M property investment
- AED 1M in savings
- AED 20,000/month income
Duration: 5 years (renewable)

6. STUDENT VISA
Enrolled at UAE university/school
Duration: 1 year (renewable with enrolment)
Note: University students can apply for part-time work permit

7. FAMILY VISA (SPONSORSHIP)
Employed/investor resident can sponsor:
- Spouse (any nationality)
- Children under 18 (unlimited)
- Children 18-25 if studying full-time
- Parents (if sponsor earns AED 20,000+/month)
Cost: AED 3,000-5,000 for family visa application

8. REMOTE WORK VISA (VIRTUAL WORKING PROGRAMME)
Work remotely for non-UAE company while living in UAE
Duration: 1 year
Requirements: Income USD 3,500+/month, remote employment, health insurance
Cost: AED 3,770 (application + processing)

9. GREEN VISA
Self-employed, skilled professionals, outstanding students
Duration: 5 years
No employer sponsor needed
Income: AED 360,000/year (skilled) or strong academic record (students)

EMIRATES ID
All UAE residents must have Emirates ID (issued by ICA — Federal Authority for Identity)
Cost: AED 100-370 depending on visa duration
Biometric: Fingerprints and photo required
Used for: Healthcare, banking, government services, property transactions

KEY CONTACTS:
- GDRFA Dubai: gdrfad.gov.ae (Dubai visa applications)
- ICA Federal: ica.gov.ae (Golden Visa, UAE-wide)
- MOHRE: mohre.gov.ae (employment visa)

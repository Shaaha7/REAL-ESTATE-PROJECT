COMPLETE PROPERTY BUYING PROCESS IN DUBAI
==========================================

STEP 1: DEFINE YOUR REQUIREMENTS AND BUDGET
- Determine total budget (property price + 7-8% transaction costs)
- Decide: Investment vs end-use, ready vs off-plan
- Choose preferred areas and property type
- If financing: Get mortgage pre-approval before searching

STEP 2: PROPERTY SEARCH AND VIEWINGS
- Work with RERA-licensed broker (verify on Dubai REST app)
- Search: Property Finder, Bayut, Dubizzle, developer websites
- Schedule viewings (typically 30-60 minute appointments)
- Compare similar properties in the area

STEP 3: MAKING AN OFFER (RESALE PROPERTIES)
- Submit verbal or written offer to seller's agent
- Negotiate price, payment terms, and handover date
- Agree on what fixtures/furniture is included

STEP 4: MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MOU)
Also called Form F (Dubai Land Department official form):
- Both parties sign the MOU/Form F
- Buyer pays deposit: Usually 10% of purchase price
- MOU states: Price, payment terms, NOC requirements, completion date
- Time frame to complete: Usually 30-60 days

STEP 5: NOC APPLICATION
- Seller applies for NOC from developer/Owners Association
- Buyer must clear all outstanding service charges on the property
- Time: 5-15 working days
- Cost: AED 500-5,000 (paid by seller usually)

STEP 6: MORTGAGE PROCESSING (IF APPLICABLE)
- Submit full application to bank with all documents
- Bank conducts property valuation (AED 2,500-3,500)
- Bank issues formal offer letter
- Review and sign mortgage documents
- Time: 2-4 weeks after full documents submitted

STEP 7: TRANSFER AT DLD
All parties attend Dubai Land Department transfer:
- Seller, buyer (or power of attorney holders)
- If mortgaged: Bank representative may attend
- Payment: Manager's cheques or bank transfer (cash not accepted)
- New title deed issued immediately or within 1-3 days

STEP 8: POST-TRANSFER
- Update DEWA (electricity and water) account: dewa.gov.ae
- Update Dubai Municipality records if villa/land
- Register tenancy on Ejari if renting out
- Register for Owners Association and pay service charges
- For off-plan: Update developer records with new ownership

TRANSACTION COSTS (BUYER):
- DLD transfer fee: 4% of purchase price
- DLD admin fee: AED 4,000 (below AED 500K) or AED 580 + AED 4,000 (above)
- Agent commission: Typically 2% (paid by buyer in Dubai)
- Mortgage arrangement fee: 0.5-1% of loan amount
- Property valuation: AED 2,500-3,500 (if mortgaged)
- Title deed: AED 500
- TOTAL APPROXIMATE COSTS: 7-8% of purchase price

TIMELINE OVERVIEW:
- Offer to MOU signing: 1-3 days
- MOU to NOC: 5-15 working days
- Mortgage approval: 2-4 weeks
- Transfer at DLD: 1 day
- Total: 4-8 weeks (cash), 6-12 weeks (mortgage)

POWER OF ATTORNEY
If buyer or seller cannot attend:
- Notarized POA required
- If signed abroad: Must be attested by UAE Embassy + Ministry of Foreign Affairs
- Cost: AED 2,000-4,000 (notarization and attestation)

UAE GOLDEN VISA — PROPERTY INVESTMENT PATHWAY
===============================================

WHAT IS THE UAE GOLDEN VISA?
The UAE Golden Visa is a long-term residence visa (5 or 10 years) that allows holders to:
- Live, work, and study in the UAE
- Stay outside UAE for extended periods without visa cancellation
- Sponsor family members and domestic staff
- No need for a UAE national sponsor

PROPERTY INVESTMENT CRITERIA
Effective 2022 changes by UAE government:

10-YEAR GOLDEN VISA (PROPERTY):
- Minimum property value: AED 2,000,000 (AED 2 million)
- Property must be fully paid (cash or completed mortgage)
- Properties under mortgage: Only qualifying if total value ≥ AED 2M AND paid portion ≥ AED 2M
- The property must be in the investor's personal name (not company name)
- Joint ownership: Eligible if individual's share ≥ AED 2M

5-YEAR GREEN VISA (PROPERTY):
- Minimum property value: AED 750,000
- Renewable every 5 years
- Slightly less benefits than 10-year Golden Visa

ELIGIBLE PROPERTY TYPES
- Residential (apartment, villa, townhouse)
- Commercial (offices, retail — if investor category)
- Must be completed/ready (off-plan generally not eligible until handover)
- Must be in UAE (any emirate)

FAMILY BENEFITS
Golden Visa holders can sponsor:
- Spouse (no restrictions on number of spouses for UAE law)
- Children (no age limit for unmarried daughters, sons up to 25 or with special needs)
- Parents
- Domestic workers (housemaids, drivers)

ADDITIONAL BENEFITS
- No sponsor required for UAE residency
- Start and own 100% of businesses in UAE mainland
- Access to UAE banking as resident
- Children can enroll in UAE schools/universities at resident rates
- Expatriate allowance for flights (if employer sponsored)

APPLICATION PROCESS
Step 1: Obtain property title deed from DLD
Step 2: Get official property valuation from DLD-approved valuator
Step 3: Apply through GDRFA (Dubai) or ICA (Federal level)
Step 4: Submit documents: Passport, title deed, valuation report, property photos
Step 5: Medical fitness test + Emirates ID application
Step 6: Golden Visa stamp in passport

PROCESSING TIME: 2-4 weeks after document submission
COST: Approximately AED 3,000-5,000 for all government fees

WHO ELSE QUALIFIES FOR GOLDEN VISA?
- Investors (AED 2M+ in UAE)
- Entrepreneurs (approved startup or AED 500K investment)
- Exceptional talents (doctors, scientists, artists, athletes)
- Outstanding students (GPA 3.75+ from top universities)
- Frontline heroes (doctors, nurses who served during COVID-19)
- Humanitarian workers

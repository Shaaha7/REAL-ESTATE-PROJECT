SHARJAH & RAS AL KHAIMAH — UAE PROPERTY MARKETS
=================================================

SHARJAH REAL ESTATE

Overview:
Sharjah is the 3rd largest emirate, directly bordering Dubai (15-25 min drive).
Known as the cultural capital of UAE. More conservative lifestyle vs Dubai.
30% cheaper than equivalent Dubai properties.

Freehold zones for foreigners (introduced 2014):
- Tilal City
- Aljada (Arada developer — mega-project)
- Nasma Residences
- Hayyan

Price ranges:
- 1BR apartment: AED 300,000 – 600,000
- 2BR apartment: AED 500,000 – 900,000
- 3BR villa: AED 900,000 – 2,000,000
- 4BR villa townhouse: AED 1,200,000 – 3,000,000

Rental yields: 6-8% gross (comparable to Dubai affordable areas)

Key developer: Arada (Aljada — 24M sqft mega-development, includes schools, retail, theme park)

Who buys in Sharjah:
- Families wanting more space for less money
- Conservative buyers preferring Islamic lifestyle
- Dubai commuters (15-25 min via Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road)
- Budget-conscious investors

Sharjah transfer fee: 4% (same as Dubai)
Sharjah regulations: Sharjah Real Estate Registration Department (SRERD)

RAS AL KHAIMAH (RAK) REAL ESTATE

Overview:
Northernmost emirate. 45-60 min from Dubai. Dramatic mountain/beach scenery.
Fastest growing real estate market in UAE 2023-2024.
Announced: Wynn Al Marjan Island Resort (UAE's first casino resort, opening 2027)

Key areas:
Al Marjan Island:
- Man-made archipelago in Arabian Gulf
- Price: AED 800,000 – 5,000,000 (apartments and villas)
- Wynn Resort effect: Prices up 60% since announcement (2022-2024)
- Best for: Short-term rental investors (tourism will surge post-2027)
- Expected yield post-Wynn opening: 12-18% holiday home

Mina Al Arab (RAK Properties):
- Waterfront community with beach access
- Villas and apartments AED 600,000 – 3,000,000
- Natural island setting, mangroves, wildlife

Hayat Island:
- New luxury launches
- AED 1,000,000 – 8,000,000

RAK transfer fee: 2% (cheaper than Dubai's 4%)
RAK yields: 6-10% long-term, 12-20% short-term (post-Wynn)

Key developers in RAK: RAK Properties, Aldar, Emaar, Sobha, Danube

Investment thesis for RAK:
- Wynn casino resort opening 2027 = major tourism catalyst
- Al Marjan Island 5-star hotel pipeline: 15+ hotels planned
- Price still 40-60% below comparable Dubai properties
- Formula 1 night race being discussed for RAK (not confirmed)
- Buy now before 2027 Wynn opening drives prices further

AJMAN REAL ESTATE (BONUS)
Ajman is the smallest emirate, cheapest in UAE.
1BR apartment: AED 200,000 – 400,000
Yields: 8-12% (highest in UAE)
Downside: Less developed, limited amenities, mostly local tenants
Best for: Pure yield investors with small budgets

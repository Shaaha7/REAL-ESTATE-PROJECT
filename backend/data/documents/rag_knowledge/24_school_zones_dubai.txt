DUBAI SCHOOL ZONES — PROPERTY AND EDUCATION GUIDE
==================================================

WHY SCHOOLS MATTER FOR PROPERTY VALUE
Properties near top-rated KHDA schools command 10-20% premium.
Many expat families choose where to live based on school availability and commute.
School year runs September to June in most Dubai schools.

DUBAI SCHOOL RATING SYSTEM
KHDA (Knowledge and Human Development Authority) rates schools:
Outstanding → Good → Acceptable → Weak → Very Weak

SCHOOL BY COMMUNITY — QUICK REFERENCE

Dubai Hills Estate: Nord Anglia (Outstanding), GEMS Wellington, American School
Arabian Ranches: JESS Ranches (Outstanding), Ranches Primary
The Springs/Meadows: Dubai British School, Dubai International Academy
JVC: multiple affordable schools, Gems World Academy (Good)
Mirdif: Mirdif American School, Uptown School, Our Own HS

Palm Jumeirah: no school on Palm — shuttle buses to mainland
JLT: GEMS Wellington Primary (Good rated)
Dubai Marina: no school in Marina — shuttle to nearby areas

SCHOOL BUS AVAILABILITY
Most international schools offer bus routes covering major communities.
Bus cost: AED 5,000-12,000 per year (additional to school fees).
Communities with strong bus coverage: Springs, Meadows, Ranches, Dubai Hills, Mirdif.
